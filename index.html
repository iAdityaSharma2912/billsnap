Questions before you download

Everything a shop owner usually asks.

No catch. BillSnap is completely free — no trial period, no premium tier, no credit card required. It runs on your own PC so there's no server cost for us to pass on. We built it for shops that shouldn't have to pay a monthly subscription just to write a bill.
Yes, completely. BillSnap runs entirely on your PC — no internet connection is ever needed, not even for setup. You can create invoices, manage inventory, and print bills even during a power cut with a laptop, or when BSNL is down. Your data never touches any server.
All your data — invoices, customers, inventory, reports — is stored in a single file on your own PC at C:\Users\YourName\AppData\Roaming\BillSnap\. Nothing is uploaded anywhere. BillSnap also has a one-click backup feature that zips everything into one file so you can save a copy to a pen drive.
Yes. BillSnap supports multiple companies on the same installation — each with its own invoice series, inventory, and reports. You just switch between them from a tab at the top of the screen. Each install of BillSnap (on a different PC) gets its own independent database, so two shops can each have their own completely separate data.
It generates a Cash Memo / Delivery Challan with your shop name, address, customer details, item list, and grand total. The PDF fits one A4 page with two copies — Original on the top half, Duplicate on the bottom half, separated by a dotted cut line. Your bank account details and UPI QR code can also be printed on it automatically.
Use the Backup feature before anything happens — it creates a single .zip file with your entire database, all invoice PDFs, and export files. When you set up a new PC, install BillSnap fresh, then use Restore to upload that backup zip and everything comes back exactly as it was.
The current version generates a Cash Memo / Delivery Challan format, which is what most small shops use for day-to-day billing. A full GST Tax Invoice format with GSTIN, HSN codes, and tax breakdowns is planned as a future update. If you need this urgently, it's worth raising as a request on our GitHub page.
BillSnap requires Windows 10 or Windows 11. It uses WebView2, which is built into Windows 10 (version 21H2 and later) and Windows 11 but is not available on Windows 7 or 8. If your shop PC is still on Windows 7 or 8, we'd recommend upgrading — Microsoft stopped security updates for both versions years ago.
